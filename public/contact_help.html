Help / Support

  • How to Book an Appointment

    Go to the "Book Appointment" page, fill in your details, select a dentist, date and time, then submit the form.

  • How to Cancel or Reschedule an Appointment

    Visit "My Appointments" page, find your appointment, and use the cancel or reschedule options.

  • How to Check Appointment Status

    Check the "My Appointments" page or dashboard for the latest status updates.

  • How to Update My Profile

    Go to the "Settings" page to update your personal information, contact details, and preferences.

  • How to Change Settings

    Access the "Settings" page to modify your account settings, notifications, and app preferences.

  • How to Change My Password

    Go to the "Settings" page and select "Change Password" to update your password.

  • How to Contact Support

    Use the contact information provided above.
