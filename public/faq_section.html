FAQ Section

How do I book an appointment?

To book an appointment, go to the "Book Appointment" page, fill out the form with your details, select a dentist, choose a date and time, then click "Book Appointment." You will receive a confirmation once the appointment is submitted.

How can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?

You can cancel or reschedule your appointment by going to the "My Appointments" page. Find the appointment you want to change, then use the provided options (cancel/reschedule). If there is no direct option, contact the clinic directly.

How do I know if my appointment is approved?

Once your appointment request is reviewed, you will see the status update on the "My Appointments" page. Approved appointments will be marked as approved.

What services does the clinic offer?

The clinic offers a range of dental services including checkups, teeth cleaning, extractions, braces consultations, and other general dental care. Check the "Type of Service" options when booking an appointment for the full list.

How will I receive appointment notifications?

Appointment notifications appear in your account dashboard. Depending on the system setup, you may also receive email notifications about booking confirmations, approval status, and reminders.
